About me

My name is Mikko Piuhola. I'm a 24-year-old Finn with a passion for all things web. I've been designing and developing beautiful things for nearly 10 years and have no desire to ever stop.

I'm currently studying for my Bachelors of Engineering in Media Technology, majoring in Digital media. My studies have given me lessons in many valuable subjects, such as software development, project management and web design.

As a student and a night-time web developer, I have a love for pixel-perfection and wonderful UI designs. Beyond the web, I'm a music lover and an AV enthusiast. I've also photographed for nearly 10 years. I'm eager to pounce on any interested opportunities presented to me, so shoot me a message if you got excited, or want to see more.
