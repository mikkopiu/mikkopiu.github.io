NCO Publication

PDF (7.6MB, Finnish)

A publication made for a course of NCOs in the Communications Regiment of the Finnish Defence Forces.

The new NCOs (corporals) have a tradition of having a course publication made, including short articles by the conscripts and lists of the course participants.

My task was to completely redesign the look and layout of the publication and deliver it to print within two weeks. The client was extremely happy with the results.

All rights for the contents and design of the publication are held by The Finnish Defence Forces. The linked publication is for demonstration purposes only.
