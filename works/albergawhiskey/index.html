Alberga Whiskey

Live site

A product site made in Flash to market an imaginary whiskey.

The site was created in Flash Professional as a courseproject. The idea was to create a Flash-based site to market an imaginary drink. All the elements were created by me in either Photoshop or Illustrator, and the video was edited in After Effects. Music consists of royalty free tracks by Kevin MacLeod.

The look and feel were heavily inspired by traditional whiskey companies, and were spun in a more fun direction.
